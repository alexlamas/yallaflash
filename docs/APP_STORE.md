## Description

Learn Lebanese Arabic with a personal AI tutor and spaced repetition.

Yalla Flash teaches you the Lebanese Arabic people actually speak -- not
textbook fusha. Chat with your tutor, add the words you want to learn, and
review them right before you'd forget them.

- A tutor that adapts to you: tell it how you like to be coached and it
  remembers.
- Spaced repetition that works: multiple choice first, then recall, then
  producing the word cold.
- Add words your way: type them, paste a list, or snap a photo of a menu or
  your notes.
- Starter packs of high-frequency words to get going fast.
- Review reminders exactly when words come due.

Yalla -- your Teta will be proud.
